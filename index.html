About the VDC

A committee run by the village, for the village.

The Bosele Village Development Committee coordinates local development, keeps residents informed, and represents Bosele's interests to the District Council.

We plan and oversee community projects — water points, roads, schools and clinics — work with ward councillors and the Kgosi's office on local governance, and organise the Kgotla meetings where the village comes together to decide on issues that affect everyone.

What we do

Services for residents

Support and administration handled through the VDC office and Kgotla.

Kgotla meetings

Community gatherings for consultation, dispute resolution, and village decisions, chaired by the Kgosi and VDC.

Land & permits

Guidance on residential plot applications and referrals to the Land Board for allocations.

Water & sanitation

Reporting borehole and standpipe issues, and coordinating with Water Utilities Corporation.

Roads & infrastructure

Tracking road maintenance requests and reporting to the District Council works department.

Youth & social programmes

Support for youth groups, farmers' committees, and vulnerable household registration.

Records & registration

Letters of introduction, indigent registration, and referrals for national documents.
